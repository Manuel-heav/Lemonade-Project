About The Game

To succeed in Project Lemonade, you have to rely on you alone, there are no numbers to help you indicate what you need, there are only success and failures. With Project Lemonade, you will develop skills, polish your attributes that will be useful to you not only in the World, but this World as well. You will grow your ability while having fun.

Project Lemonade will take place in Discord, Reddit and Our very own website, you can easily play the game that involves the most amount of people you have ever been in at the same time, with dreams, dedication and determination, we will make something truly special. A story that for years to come documented by YouTubers and Redditors alike.

Project Lemonade is currently a project in development, we are developing Systems of tools which needs designers, a website and discord/reddit bot that needs developers, we would appreciate every and all help we can get, you would get us closer to Project Lemonade's success.
